About Me

Hello again! My name is Eric Hunt and I am a recent graduate of CUNY Queens College, with a Bachelor’s of Art in Computer Science. I love coding and bodybuilding! I am well versed in multiple programming languages, primarily in Java. However, I feel that my calling is in Web Design and Development. As a hobby, I use the HTML skills I’ve harnessed during my academic career to create and design my own templates. Using HTML5 & CSS3, my work focuses on clean and responsive designs that can easily be viewed and navigated on multiple devices. I believe the implementation of a flexible design is essential in today’s market
